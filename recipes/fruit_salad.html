Fruit Salad

What is Fruit Salad?

Fruit salad is a sort of salad made from a variety of fruits all chopped to a similar size. Fruit salads are versatile and can be made from almost any fruit that can be served in small sizes.

Ingredients

  • Fruits (any amount of variety of fruit)

How to Prepare

  1. Prepare all fruits by peeling or washing
  2. Using a sharp knife, chop fruit into medium sized pieces
  3. Add all chopped fruits to bowl
  4. Mix fruits in bowl using a large spoon
  5. Serve fruit salad in smaller bowls, or mixing bowl
